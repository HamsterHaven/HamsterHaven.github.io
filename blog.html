Welcome to Hamster Haven

  • Grace Barnett-Fitch

What began as a small passion project for animal lovers has grown into a trusted hub for hamster care, education, and advocacy. We share reliable information on everything from choosing the right breed and setting up a cozy habitat to understanding hamster behavior and nutrition.

Our mission is to create a community where both new and experienced owners can learn, connect, and care for their pets with confidence and compassion.
