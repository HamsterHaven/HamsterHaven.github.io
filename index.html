    Which one is right for me?

    Getting started as a hamster owner begins with finding the right match for you. Every hamster breed has its own unique traits, and choosing one that fits your lifestyle can make all the difference in how happy both you and your pet will be. Some hamsters are more active and curious, needing lots of space and playtime, while others are calm and enjoy gentle handling.

    • Some breeds love to explore and run, while others are quieter and easier to handle.
    • Syrians need to live alone, while some dwarf species can share space.
    • Sand baths are important! Never use water!

    It’s important to think about how much time you can spend with your hamster, how much room you have for a cage, and whether you want one that prefers living alone or in pairs. Taking the time to learn about each breed helps you start your journey with confidence and gives your new companion the best chance at a comfortable, loving home.
